Top Heroes
1 Mickey Mouse
First Appearance: November 18, 1928
Created by: Walt Disney, Ub Iwerks
Voice: Walt Disney (1928-1947), Jimmy MacDonald (1947-1977), Wayne Allwine (1977-2009), Bret Iwan (2009-present)
Character Traits: Cheerful, adventurous, optimistic, determined
Movie: Steamboat Willie and numerous others
Story: Mickey Mouse, Disney's mascot, embarks on countless adventures with his friends, spreading joy and laughter worldwide.
2 Mulan
First Appearance: June 19, 1998
Created by: Robert D. San Souci (author), Barry Cook, Tony Bancroft (directors)
Voice: Ming-Na Wen (speaking), Lea Salonga (singing)
Character Traits: Brave, determined, resourceful, selfless
Movie: Mulan
Story: Mulan disguises herself as a man to take her father's place in the army and becomes one of China's greatest heroes.
3 Simba
First Appearance: June 15, 1994
Created by: Roger Allers, Rob Minkoff
Voice: Matthew Broderick (adult), Jonathan Taylor Thomas (young)
Character Traits: Brave, adventurous, determined, loyal
Movie: The Lion King
Story: Simba, a young lion cub, must find his place as king of the Pride Lands after facing loss and adversity.
